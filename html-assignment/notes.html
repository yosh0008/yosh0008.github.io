5 HTML Tags I learned

<p>
Adds a paragraph of text.
<img>
Inserts a photo.
<footer>
Inserts a footer at the end of your web page. It typically contains important information, such as copyright, disclaimers, and contact info.
<h1> to <h6>
Creates headings in your web page. <h1> being the most important and <h6> being the least important.
<em>
Emphasizes selected text.
